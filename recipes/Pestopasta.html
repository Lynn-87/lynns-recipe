Pesto Pasta

Description

This recipe is my favorite! It's delicious and its easy to make.

i'm in love with pasta and pesto so the combination of those two ingredients are just to die for.

It can be uses as a main course or side dish.

Ingredients for 4

  • ⅔ (16 ounce) package corkscrew-shaped pasta (fusilli)
  • ½ cup chopped fresh basil
  • ½ cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 3 ½ tablespoons and ⅙ teaspoon pine nuts
  • 3 ½ tablespoons and ⅙ teaspoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • ⅔ clove garlic, diced
  • ¼ cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoons finely diced red onion
  • ¼ cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoons finely diced radicchio
  • ½ tablespoon and ½ teaspoon mayonnaise, or to taste
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

how to make this dish

  • Step 1:

    Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook fusilli in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes. Drain.

  • Step 2:

    Combine basil, Parmesan cheese, pine nuts, oil, and garlic in a food processor; blend to make pesto.

  • Step 3:

    Toss pasta with pesto and mix in red onion, radicchio, and mayonnaise. Season with salt and pepper. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.
